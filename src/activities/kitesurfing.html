Kitesurfing

What is Kitesurfing?

Kitesurfing is a wind-powered water surface sport where you stand on a board and use a kite to move across the surface of the water. The kite uses the wind to propel you forward.

If you would like to see an animated demonstration, you can check out this video!

Skills & Attributes for Kitesurfing

  • Relatively good fitness (especially in the core)
  • Swimming ability
  • Good confidence in yourself & trying new things
  • The ability to remain calm

Learning to Kitesurf

When learning to Kitesurf with Adventure Cymru, you will go through 3 steps

  1. Flying a kite on land (launching, landing & controlling a kite).
  2. Flying a kite in water (body dragging, control & re-launching)
  3. Standing on & riding a board in the water

You can also take a quick look at this WikiHow article for some extra tips and advice when starting out.

These steps will also give you experience in:

  • Assessing an area to ensure it is safe to kite
  • Understanding wind direction
  • Understanding tides, currents & hazards
  • Choosing the right equipment
  • Setting-up, tuning & packing away equipment
  • Safety procedures
  • What to do in case on an emergency
  • Board-starts (standing on the board)
  • Continuous riding
  • Speed control
  • Rules & right-of-ways on the water

What Equipment do I Need to Kitesurf?

  • A kite
  • A control bar
  • A seat (beginners) or waist harness (more advanced)
  • A safety leash
  • A large kite board
  • A helmet
  • Water shoes
  • A wetsuit
