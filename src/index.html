Helo! We're Adventure Cymru.

Who are we?

Adventure Cymru is Wales' favourite outdoor centre. We offer a wide range of activities, kite-surfing, kayaking and more.

Recently, Adventure Cymru won a grant! This has allowed us to run residential courses and allow our visitors to experience an even wider range of activities over several-day-long visits! These multi-day visits help build confidence and get people moving!

By far, our customer-favourite activity is Kitesurfing. But, if that doesn't tickle your fancy, how about taking a look at our other activities?
